Project manager responsibilities towards the team
- He should know how to communicate with them and study their personalities for all team members.
- He should make a motivation environment to encourage the team members to work and improve their productivity.
- He should have an experience in tasks distribution and who is the most suitable member for a specific task
- He should take in consideration their social life that may affects their productivity.
- He should make sure that members relationship is based on respect.
- He should make sure that each task has its right time period to avoid the pressure and low quality
